PLACE YOUR BRAND IMAGES IN THIS FOLDER
======================================

1) LOGO  ->  name it exactly:   logo.png
   Final path: images/logo.png
   - Transparent-background PNG, roughly square, ~200x200 to 512x512 px.
   - Your golden-brown brand mark. It appears in the header AND footer of every page.
   - Until this file exists, the site shows a gold "G" placeholder automatically.

2) FAVICON  ->  name it exactly:   favicon.png
   Final path: images/favicon.png
   - Square PNG (512x512 px recommended). Shown on the browser tab.
   - A simplified version of your logo works best.

3) (Optional) Your own photos: add them here (e.g. hero.jpg) and update the
   image src="..." links in the HTML files. See README.md for details.
