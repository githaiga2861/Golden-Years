Certified Home Care Aide Training

Empowering caregivers with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to deliver excellent, safe, and compassionate care.

Program Overview

We Don't Just Train Caregivers — We Develop Care Professionals

Great care begins with great training. Our Certified Home Care Aide (HCA) training program prepares aspiring and current caregivers to deliver safe, skilled, and genuinely compassionate support in the home. Built and supervised by experienced nursing leadership, the program blends practical, hands-on instruction with the professionalism and ethics that define exceptional caregiving.

Whether you're starting a new career in caregiving, supporting a family member, or building on existing experience, our training gives you the foundation to provide care you can be proud of — and that families can trust.

  • Practical, skills-based instructionLearn by doing, with techniques you'll use every day in the home.
  • Nurse-led curriculumDeveloped and overseen by our registered-nurse leadership.
  • Certification preparationDesigned to help prepare you for Home Care Aide certification.
Curriculum

What You'll Learn

Personal Care Techniques

Safe, dignified assistance with bathing, dressing, grooming, toileting, and mobility.

Safety & Fall Prevention

Identifying hazards, safe transfers, and preventing the falls that endanger older adults.

Dementia & Alzheimer's Care

Communication strategies and compassionate approaches for memory-care clients.

Infection Control

Hand hygiene, standard precautions, and practices that keep clients and caregivers safe.

Communication & Professionalism

Building trust, respecting boundaries, and working as part of a care team.

Abuse Prevention & Reporting

Recognizing, preventing, and properly reporting abuse, neglect, and exploitation.

Medication Assistance Basics

Foundational, compliant support for medication reminders and assistance.

ADL Support Techniques

Hands-on help with the activities of daily living that keep clients independent.
