Professional Skilled Nursing Services

Our registered and licensed nurses bring clinical expertise into the comfort of your home — so healing and ongoing care can happen where you're most at ease.

Overview

Hospital-Quality Nursing, Delivered at Home

Skilled nursing brings the clinical judgment and hands-on expertise of a licensed nurse directly to the home. It's ideal for individuals recovering from surgery or hospitalization, managing complex or chronic conditions, or requiring treatments and monitoring that go beyond what personal care alone can provide.

At Golden Years, every skilled-nursing client receives an individualized, physician-informed care plan developed and overseen by our nursing leadership — including our founder, a BSN-prepared Registered Nurse. We collaborate closely with your doctors and specialists, document carefully, and adjust the plan as your needs change.

Who benefits from skilled nursing? Clients who need wound care, injections, infusion or catheter support, careful medication management, disease monitoring, or a safe, supervised transition home after a hospital stay.

What's Included

Our Skilled Nursing Capabilities

🩹

Wound Care

Assessment, cleaning, dressing changes, and monitoring of surgical, pressure, diabetic, and chronic wounds to promote healing and prevent infection.

💊

Medication Management

Administration, reconciliation, and monitoring of medications, with vigilance for interactions and side effects.

🩸

Blood Glucose & Insulin

Blood glucose monitoring and insulin administration for clients managing diabetes safely at home.

❤️‍🩹

Chronic Disease Management

Ongoing nursing support for diabetes, heart disease, COPD, and other long-term conditions.

🏥

Post-Hospitalization Care

Transitional care that bridges hospital to home — reducing readmissions and supporting a safe recovery.

📈

Health Status Monitoring

Regular assessment of vital signs and overall condition to catch changes early and act quickly.

🧪

Catheter & G-Tube Support

Skilled catheter care and enteral (G-tube) feeding support, with delegation to trained aides where appropriate.

📚

Patient & Family Education

Teaching clients and families how to manage conditions, medications, and equipment confidently at home.

🚪

RN Visitation

Scheduled nurse visits for assessment, care-plan oversight, and supervision of delegated caregivers.

Rehabilitation

Therapy Services at Home

Recovery and independence often depend on skilled therapy. Through our team of licensed therapists, we provide rehabilitation in the comfort of home:

  • Physical Therapy (PT)Rebuilds strength, balance, and mobility; reduces fall risk and supports recovery after injury or surgery.
  • Occupational Therapy (OT)Restores the skills needed for daily living and adapts the home for safety and independence.
  • Speech Therapy (ST)Addresses speech, communication, and swallowing difficulties after stroke, illness, or surgery.
Clinical Leadership

Care You Can Trust

Our skilled nursing program is led by Rose Mbote, BSN, RN, RND, PDN, founder and CEO of Golden Years. As a Registered Nurse, Registered Nurse Delegator, and experienced Private Duty Nurse, she brings the clinical depth needed to oversee complex, high-acuity care at home.

Every nurse and aide who joins our team is carefully screened and background-checked, oriented to our standards of practice, and supervised on an ongoing basis. Skilled tasks are performed or overseen by licensed staff, and delegated tasks follow Washington State's Nurse Delegation requirements. The result is care that is not only compassionate, but clinically sound and accountable.
