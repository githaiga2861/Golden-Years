Why People Choose Us

Why So Many Caring People Find Their Way to Golden Years

At Golden Years Home Health Supported Living, care flows in every direction. We're a nurse-led team rooted in Sumner and serving families across Pierce, King, Thurston, Lewis, and Pacific Counties — and we believe the people who care for our clients deserve to be cared for too. Here, you'll find hands-on training, genuine mentorship from experienced nurses, schedules that respect your life, and the deep satisfaction of helping someone live with dignity in their own home. Compassionate people find their way to us because they want work with meaning — and they stay because they finally feel supported in doing it.
