Welcome to Golden Years

More than a care agency — a partner in your loved one's wellbeing.

Choosing care for yourself or a family member is one of the most important decisions you will ever make. At Golden Years Home Health Supported Living LLC, we understand the weight of that decision — and we meet it with clinical excellence, genuine compassion, and unwavering respect for the dignity of every person we serve.

Founded and led by Rose Mbote, BSN, RN, RND, PDN, our agency is built on a registered-nurse foundation. That means every personal-care plan, every delegated task, and every caregiver assignment is shaped by clinical judgment and supervised by a licensed professional. We bring the standard of care you would expect from a hospital into the comfort and familiarity of home.

Whether your loved one needs skilled nursing after a hospital stay, daily assistance with the activities of living, or ongoing support to remain independent at home, we design a care plan around the person — never the other way around.

What We Provide

Our Comprehensive Home Health Services

From skilled clinical nursing to everyday personal support, Golden Years delivers the full spectrum of home-based care — all coordinated and supervised by a registered nurse.

Skilled Nursing

RN and LPN clinical care at home — wound care, medication management, blood glucose monitoring, and post-hospital recovery support.

Learn More

Personal Care & ADLs

Dignified, hands-on help with bathing, dressing, grooming, mobility, toileting, and the daily activities of living.

Learn More

Nurse Delegation

A licensed RN delegates and oversees specific clinical tasks to certified aides under Washington's Nurse Delegation Program.

Learn More

Respite Care

Short-term, dependable relief for family caregivers so you can rest, travel, or simply recharge with peace of mind.

Learn More

Supported Living

Ongoing assistance that helps individuals live as independently and safely as possible in their own home or community setting.

Learn More

Chronic Disease Management

Coordinated support for diabetes, heart disease, COPD, and other long-term conditions to prevent crises and hospital visits.

Learn More
